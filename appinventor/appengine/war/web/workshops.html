Workshops

As part of the training phase of the method proposed, several workshops with lectures and students from the University of Cádiz were conducted. The duration of each workshop was five hours and they began with a brief introduction to educational applications of mobile devices and AR foundations. Following this, the use of the authoring tool was explained. To reinforce and consolidate what they learnt, each participant developed some educational mobile apps by themselves
