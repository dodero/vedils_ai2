Implement the students' spatial vision and motivate them with innovative activities





Displays 3D figures in Augmented Reality (RA)

auRA 3D is an app for playing three-dimensional figures in Augmented Reality (RA). The simple design allows an intuitive interface for this application to be used in teaching. auRA 3D allows autocorrection of 8 different exercises of figures in elevation, plant and profile. The student can enlarge and reduce the figure in RA, as well as rotate it 360⁰ so that all angles can be appreciated and the recognition of hidden lines easier.



Implementation of VEDILS

Components of VEDILS
  • ARCamera to show the Augmented Reality
  • ARObjectTracker to recognize images
  • AR3DModelAsset to show 3D models
