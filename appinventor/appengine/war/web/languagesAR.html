Improving learning processes with AR





Wer bin ich?

Works like the famous game "Who am I?" and is used to help students learn German. To this end each student must first select a marker of Augmented Reality which is associated to a person, animal or object and then guess its name. To perfom the given task students are asked to work in small teams (2-3 students) providing each other with the necessary information.



Implementation of VEDILS

Components of VEDILS
  • ARCamera to show the Augmented Reality
  • ARMarkerTracker to recognize marker. Each student puts his marker on his chest.
  • ARImageAsset to show 2D images representing different people, etc.
  • ActivityTracker to to trace back who has used the app, where, when, how many times, etc.
